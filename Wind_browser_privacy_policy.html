Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

Bd ProTech operates the Wind Browser mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

1. Information Collection and Use

Wind Browser is designed with a "Privacy First" approach. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, or physical address.

  • Browsing Data: Your history, bookmarks, and open tabs are stored locally on your device. We cannot access this data.
  • Search History: Any search queries you perform are handled by the search engine you choose (e.g., Google) and are not monitored by us.

2. Permissions and Their Purpose

To provide a seamless browsing experience, Wind Browser requires the following permissions:

  • INTERNET: Required to access the World Wide Web and load websites.
  • READ/WRITE EXTERNAL STORAGE: Used to download files and save them directly to your device's storage.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE (Data Sync): Used by our background download manager to ensure your files continue downloading even if the app is minimized.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Used to show real-time download progress and completion alerts.

3. Third-Party Services

Wind Browser may use third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Search: Used for processing web queries and providing search suggestions.
  • AdMob: We may display advertisements to support the development of the app. AdMob may use cookies or advertising IDs to serve relevant ads.

4. Data Security

Since the majority of your data (history, bookmarks) is stored on your device, its security depends on your device's protection settings. We recommend using a secure screen lock on your mobile device.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
